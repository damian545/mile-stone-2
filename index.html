About Me

Hello, let me start by saying I have only been coding for 7 months. I have just recently finished an intense coding bootcamp and graduated, which I am most proud of mind you. I am looking to begin my journey down the web designing path, freelance and part time while I am still working fulltime. My background is manual handling in a factory called Monroe, they make replacement shock absorbers for cars, trucks and trains. As well as tubing for exhausts and ARB bull bars. I love designing things, I love art too funny enough and I started drawing when I was 7 years old. So designing things is really normal for me, I have also dabbled in painting cars, I have built 2 drift cars of my own in the past, I was an avid skateboarder for 20 years, I built a retaining wall at my first house all by myself 6 years ago. So as you can see, I am not afraid to try new things and get my hands dirty. But the main thing is, I am definately a creater and I am very fussy when I do create. So this is just a little bit of me, so you can understand what motivates me. Thank you for your time.
